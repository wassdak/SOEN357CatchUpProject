## Research question (template)

- **RQ:** Does an AI-assisted academic recovery planner (Version B) reduce perceived stress and/or increase exam-preparedness confidence compared to a manual planner (Version A), for students facing a standardized backlog scenario?

## Hypothesis (template)

- **H1 (example):** Participants who use Version B will report **lower perceived stress** (or comparable stress with higher confidence—specify your exact directional prediction) than those who use Version A after completing the same scenario.
- **H2 (example):** Participants who use Version B will report **higher exam-preparedness confidence** than those who use Version A.
